Accessing information about government services, welfare schemes, and community opportunities is still difficult for many people. ğŸ’¡ Our Idea
The AI Community Access Assistant is an AI-powered system designed to make public information easy to find and easy to understand. It helps users discover government schemes, public services, learning and job opportunities, and also keeps them informed about local community announcements such as events and awareness drives.
The focus of the solution is simple language, inclusive design, and easy access, so that even first-time or non-technical users can benefit from it.
